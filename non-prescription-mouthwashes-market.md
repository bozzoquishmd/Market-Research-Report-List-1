Alcoholic mouthwashes typically contain ethanol, providing a strong antiseptic effect that helps kill bacteria and freshen breath. However, they can cause dryness and irritation in some users. Non-alcoholic mouthwashes, on the other hand, often use alternative active ingredients, making them gentler and more suitable for those with sensitive mouths or seeking a milder option. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences and oral health needs.</p></p>
