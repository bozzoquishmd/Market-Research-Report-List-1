<p><p>The civil infrared detectors market includes two main types: uncooled and cooled infrared detectors. Uncooled infrared detectors operate at ambient temperatures, making them smaller and less expensive, ideal for applications like surveillance and building monitoring. In contrast, cooled infrared detectors require refrigeration to reduce noise, offering higher sensitivity and resolution, suited for military, aerospace, and high-performance industrial applications. Both technologies play crucial roles in thermal imaging, enabling effective monitoring and detection in various civil applications.</p></p>
