<p><p>The Automatic Transmission Fluid (ATF) market is segmented into semi-synthetic and full-synthetic fluids. Semi-synthetic ATF is a blend of mineral and synthetic oils, offering improved performance and protection compared to conventional fluids, making it suitable for various vehicles. Full-synthetic ATF, on the other hand, is entirely composed of synthetic base oils, providing superior lubrication, thermal stability, and extended service life. Both types cater to the growing demand for enhanced efficiency and longer transmission lifespan in modern vehicles, driving market growth.</p></p>
