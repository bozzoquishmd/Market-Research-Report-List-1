<p><p>Post-CMP cleaning market comprises two primary types: acid and alkaline materials. Acid materials, such as sulfuric or hydrochloric acid, effectively remove metal ions and contaminants from semiconductor surfaces, enhancing surface cleanliness and preventing defects. In contrast, alkaline materials, like ammonium hydroxide, are used to dissolve organic residues, providing an efficient cleaning process for various substrates. Both types are critical in ensuring high-quality results in semiconductor manufacturing, helping to maintain device performance and yield in advanced technology nodes.</p></p>
