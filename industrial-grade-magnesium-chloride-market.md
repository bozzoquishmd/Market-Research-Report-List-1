<p><p>The industrial-grade magnesium chloride market primarily comprises two types: anhydrous magnesium chloride and hexahydrate magnesium chloride. Anhydrous magnesium chloride, with no water content, is used in applications requiring high purity and chemical stability, such as de-icing and dust control. In contrast, hexahydrate magnesium chloride contains water and is often utilized in agricultural and food industries, offering moisture retention properties. Both forms cater to diverse industrial needs, emphasizing their unique properties and applications in various sectors.</p></p>
