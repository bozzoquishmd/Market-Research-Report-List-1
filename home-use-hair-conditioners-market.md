<p><p>The home use hair conditioners market includes three main types: rinse-out conditioners, leave-in conditioners, and deep conditioners. Deep conditioners, often used weekly, penetrate the hair shaft for intensive repair and nourishment, targeting damage and dryness for enhanced hair health.</p></p>
