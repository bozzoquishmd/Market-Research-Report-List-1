<p><p>Industrial tomography systems encompass various technologies for non-invasive measurement and imaging of materials and processes. Electrical Impedance Tomography (EIT) and Electrical Resistance Tomography (ERT) focus on visualizing electrical properties of materials. Electrical Capacitance Tomography (ECT) uses capacitance to determine the distribution of materials. Ultrasound systems employ sound waves to create images of internal structures. Densitometers measure the density of materials, providing critical data in industries like oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and food processing for monitoring and quality control.</p></p>
