<p><p>The Gunn diode is a type of semiconductor device that generates microwave frequency signals through a phenomenon known as negative differential resistance. It operates in a mode where an increase in voltage leads to a decrease in current, making it suitable for applications in oscillators and high-frequency amplifiers. These diodes are particularly advantageous in radar, wireless communication, and satellite communications due to their ability to handle high power levels and operate in extreme conditions.</p><p>The Gunn Diode Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.00% during the forecast period.
