<p><p>Blow Fill Seal (BFS) technology is an automated process used primarily for packaging liquid products, such as pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and food items. The process involves blowing a pre-formed container from a thermoplastic material, filling it with the product, and sealing it, all in a single continuous operation. This method enhances product safety, extends shelf life, and offers efficient packaging solutions with reduced contamination risks.</p><p>The Blow Fill Seal BFS Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.7% during the forecast period.
