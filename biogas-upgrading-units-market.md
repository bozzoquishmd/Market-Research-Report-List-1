<p><p>Biogas upgrading units are essential for transforming raw biogas into higher-quality biomethane. The market primarily comprises three types: Membrane Separation, Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA), and Other methods. Membrane separation uses selective permeable membranes to separate methane from impurities, offering efficiency and low energy consumption. PSA employs pressure cycles to adsorb unwanted gases while retaining methane. Other methods encompass various technologies like chemical scrubbing and cryogenic processes, providing alternatives based on specific application needs and economic factors.</p></p>
