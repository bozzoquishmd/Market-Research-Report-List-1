<p><p>Laser light cables are categorized into two primary types: multimode beam and single-mode beam. Multimode beam cables support multiple light pathways, allowing for a broader range of data transmission over shorter distances, making them ideal for applications within buildings or local networks. In contrast, single-mode beam cables utilize a single light pathway, enabling higher bandwidth and longer transmission distances, which is suitable for telecommunications and long-range data communication. Both types play crucial roles in modern fiber optic networks.</p></p>
