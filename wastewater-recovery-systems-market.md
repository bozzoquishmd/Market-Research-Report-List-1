<p><p>Wastewater recovery systems encompass various technologies aimed at treating and reusing wastewater. Reverse osmosis employs a semi-permeable membrane to remove contaminants, producing clean water. Membrane filtration uses membranes to separate solids from liquids, enhancing clarity and quality. Activated carbon filtration utilizes carbon to adsorb organic compounds and impurities, significantly improving water taste and safety. Media filtration involves passing water through various filter media to remove suspended particles. Each type plays a crucial role in efficient wastewater management and resource recovery.</p></p>
