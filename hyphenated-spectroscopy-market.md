<p><p>Hyphenated spectroscopy encompasses advanced analytical techniques combining two or more separation methods with detection systems. In the Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (GC-MS) market, volatile compounds are separated through gas chromatography and identified using mass spectrometry, making it ideal for environmental and forensic analysis. Conversely, the Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (LC-MS) market focuses on separating liquid samples, which are then analyzed via mass spectrometry, suitable for pharmaceuticals, biochemistry, and complex organic mixtures, enabling detailed compound characterization and quantification.</p></p>
