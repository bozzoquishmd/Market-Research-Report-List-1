<p><p>The cellulose electrode market is categorized into two main types: pure cellulose electrodes and mixed type cellulose electrodes. Pure cellulose electrodes are composed entirely of cellulose, offering high biocompatibility and structural integrity, making them ideal for specific applications in electrochemistry and biosensors. On the other hand, mixed type cellulose electrodes combine cellulose with other materials to enhance conductivity and performance, expanding their utility in various industries, including energy storage and environmental sensing. Both types cater to unique functional and performance requirements.</p></p>
