<p><strong>The Glass Coatings for Containers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot-end Coatings</li><li>Cold-end Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass coatings for containers are primarily categorized into hot-end and cold-end coatings. Hot-end coatings are applied during the glass manufacturing process while the glass is still molten, enhancing thermal resistance and surface durability. These coatings improve barrier properties, reducing interaction with contents. Cold-end coatings, on the other hand, are applied after the glass has cooled, providing additional benefits such as anti-scratch properties, UV protection, and aesthetic enhancements. Both types aim to improve the performance and longevity of glass containers.</p></p>
