They comprise thin metal reeds sealed inside a glass tube, which close or open when subjected to a magnetic field. <p><p>The High Voltage Reed Switch market comprises three main types: Form A, Form B, and Form C. Form A switches are single-pole, normally open devices that close the circuit when activated. Form B switches are single-pole, normally closed, opening the circuit upon activation. Form C switches are double-pole devices featuring both normally open and normally closed configurations, providing versatile control options. These switches are widely used in various applications, including automation, telecommunications, and power management systems.</p></p>
