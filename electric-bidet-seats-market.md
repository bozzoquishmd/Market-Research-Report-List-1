<p><ul><li>Tank</li><li>Tankless</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric bidet seats come in three market types: tank, tankless, and hybrid. Tank models feature a built-in reservoir that provides a limited amount of heated water, ensuring a consistent temperature but requiring periodic refills. Tankless models heat water on demand, offering endless, instant warmth without the need for a reservoir, making them more efficient. Hybrid options combine both systems, providing the advantages of immediate heating while maintaining a small tank for enhanced comfort during extended use. Each type caters to different user preferences and bathroom setups.</p></p>
