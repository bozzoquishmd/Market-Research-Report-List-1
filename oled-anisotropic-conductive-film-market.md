<p><p>OLED Anisotropic Conductive Film (ACF) is a specialized adhesive material used primarily in the assembly of OLED displays. It enables electrical connections between the display's driver ICs and the substrate, ensuring efficient energy transfer while maintaining flexibility and transparency.
