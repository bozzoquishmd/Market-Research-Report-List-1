<p><p>The active filters market is categorized into AC and DC active filters, each serving distinct applications. AC active filters are designed to manage power quality issues, such as harmonics and reactive power in AC systems, enhancing efficiency and stability in industrial and commercial settings. Conversely, DC active filters focus on smoothing voltage waveforms and reducing ripple in DC applications, often used in renewable energy systems and electric vehicles. Both types are essential for modern electronic systems, ensuring optimal performance and reliability.</p></p>
