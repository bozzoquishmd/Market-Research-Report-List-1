<p><p>The pneumatic vise grips market is categorized into single-action and double-action types. Single-action vise grips utilize air pressure to perform one function, typically gripping or holding an object. They offer simplicity and efficiency for basic tasks. In contrast, double-action vise grips operate with air pressure in both directions, allowing for more complex movements, such as opening and closing simultaneously. This versatility makes double-action grips ideal for demanding applications, enhancing productivity in various industrial settings.</p></p>
