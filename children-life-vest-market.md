<p><p>The children’s life vest market features three main types: foam vests, inflatable vests, and hybrid vests. Foam vests are typically made from buoyant materials that provide reliable flotation without needing inflation, making them easy to use and maintain. Inflatable vests offer compact storage and lightweight design, inflating upon immersion in water for enhanced buoyancy. Hybrid vests combine both features, offering foam for primary flotation and inflatable components for added safety, catering to varying water activity needs and safety preferences for children.</p></p>
