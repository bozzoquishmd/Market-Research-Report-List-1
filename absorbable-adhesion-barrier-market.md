<p><p>The absorbable adhesion barrier market is categorized into three main types: film formulation, gel formulation, and liquid formulation. Film formulation involves thin sheets that create a protective layer over tissues, preventing adhesions post-surgery. Gel formulation consists of viscous fluids that can easily conform to surgical sites, providing a moist environment while reducing adhesion formation. Liquid formulation, typically in a more fluid state, can be sprayed or applied directly into surgical areas, facilitating coverage and effective barrier properties, thus minimizing tissue scarring and adhesion risks.</p></p>
