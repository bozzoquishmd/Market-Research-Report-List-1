<p><p>The biopharma buffer market is categorized into several types based on their chemical composition and application. Phosphate buffers are widely used for biological systems due to their physiological relevance. Acetate buffers provide excellent pH stability in enzymatic reactions. TRIS buffers are popular in molecular biology for their compatibility with nucleic acids and proteins. Other types include citrate and carbonate buffers, catering to specific needs in therapeutic formulations and research applications. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing biopharmaceutical processes.</p></p>
