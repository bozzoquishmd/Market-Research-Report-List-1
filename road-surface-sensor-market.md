<p><strong>The Road Surface Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The road surface sensor market is divided into two primary types: active and passive sensors. Active sensors emit signals, such as lasers or infrared, to detect road conditions and provide real-time data on surface conditions, while passive sensors rely on existing signals, like those from vehicles or environmental factors, to gather information. Active sensors typically offer more precise data but can be more expensive, whereas passive sensors are often more cost-effective and easier to implement but might provide less detailed insights.</p></p>
